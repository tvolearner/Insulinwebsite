Admelog [SoloStar, Vial]
Generic: Insulin lispro (analog, conventional) 		4 - 6 hours
(or 3 - 5 hours)
Indications: Type 1 and Type 2 ("meal-time" insulin)
MOA: Lower blood glucose by inhibiting hepatic glucose production
Dosing (Adult): 0.5 units/kg/day (Type 1)/ 4 - 6 units subcutaneously daily (Type 2)
Side Effects: pain and redness at the injection site, low blood sugar
Monitoring: Check blood sugar levels, check A1C every 3 - 6 months
