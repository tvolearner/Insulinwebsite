Medication Name Duration Information
Humulin N (U-100) [KwikPen, Vial]
Generic: Insulin isophane or NPH (human) 		14 - 18 hours
Indications: Type 1 and Type 2 (basal insulin)
MOA: Lower blood glucose by inhibiting hepatic glucose production
Dosing (Adult): 0.2 - 0.4 units/kg/day (Type 1)/ 10 units subcutaneously daily (Type 2)
Side Effects: pain and redness at the injection site, low blood sugar
Monitoring: Check blood sugar levels, check A1C every 3 - 6 months
Humulin R (U-500) [KwikPen, Vial]
Generic: Regular Insulin (human) 		~ 21 hours (13-24 hours)
Indications: Type 1 and Type 2 (basal insulin)
MOA: Lower blood glucose by inhibiting hepatic glucose production
Dosing (Adult): 0.2 - 0.4 units/kg/day (Type 1)/ 5 units subcutaneously twice daily (Type 2)
Side Effects: pain and redness at the injection site, low blood sugar
Monitoring: Check blood sugar levels, check A1C every 3 - 6 months
